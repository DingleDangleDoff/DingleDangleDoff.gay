Only 2 Colours.#483E48 and #70FD6E

Can have either 2 eyes or 4 eyes. If unsure what one to draw just ask. Long prehensile tail and digitigrade legs with paws. Non binary so draw as andrygynous. Anything green is bioluminecent.

Floofs on the Heel, Elbow, Chest, and Neck with green tips or a gradient to green. Floof also on the crotch and tail base but no green floofs on those.

Head shape silimar to a fluffy dragon with big ears and thin nose. Nose is halfway between canine and feline in size and shape.

Maw has maw spikes on top and bottom of mouth near the front. When mouth is closed they overlap and only top spikes are visible. Depending on art style you may or may not include teeth if you wish

The eyes are single colour with no pupil.
